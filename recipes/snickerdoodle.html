Snickerdoodles

Description

A simple and yet effective recipe for mouth-watering Snickerdoodle cookies! This unique recipe offers its users a one of a kind taste to this staple cookie.

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Potassium Bitartrate
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar for 4-5 minutes
  2. Use Cream of Tartar as a leavening agent
  3. Double roll the cookie balls into a cinnamon-sugar mixture
  4. Bake just until the edges begin to become a light golden color
